Legal Aid Eligibility

To check if you are eligible for legal aid please follow this link which will take you to the government website.

Once you have clicked on the link it will take you to a page where you can find out if you are eligible for legal aid. Please follow these steps: 

  • Click on “Start now” 
  • Click on “Housing” 
  • Click on “You’re living in rented accommodation” 
  • Click on “Eviction” 
  • It will say that you need to check if you are financially eligible for legal aid
  • Click on “Check if you qualify financially”
  • Please complete all the questions
  • If it says that you are likely to be eligible for legal aid, it will advise you to contact Civil Legal Advice, but that is not necessary:

1) We suggest that you find a solicitor or a law centre that provides legal aid to help you determine if you have legal aid and help you with your case.

2) To find law centres and housing solicitors near you please click on Legal Aid Housing Solicitors

3) Once you have completed these steps, please click on Generate Report to provide the necessary information to create a report and present to court.

  • If it says that you are unlikely to be eligible for legal aid, then please click on Generate Report. This will allow you to provide the necessary information to create a report and present to court. 
  • You may wish to talk to Civil Legal Advice if you cannot get a solicitor or a duty solicitor
